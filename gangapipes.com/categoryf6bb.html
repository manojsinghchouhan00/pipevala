Drip Irrigation System

We offer superior quality Drip Irrigation System, owing to the dependency of India agriculture on water for the purpose of irrigation.

  • Specification

 

    Emitting Pipes (IS 13488 : 2008)
  • Range :
    Dia. 12mm(2/4 LPH CL-II), 16mm(2/4 LPH CL-II) & 20mm(2/4 LPH CL-I & II)
  • Application :
    In Drip Irrigation Systems for close spacing crops.

 

We offer superior quality drip irrigation system, owing to the dependency of India agriculture on water for the purpose of irrigation. With traditional flood irrigation farmers are able to irrigate only a part of their valuable cultivated land, therefore at this juncture our product comes to wide usage. We provide the best and controlled irrigation to the cultivated land of the farmers through the most economical way of water management.

 

    Lateral Pipes (IS 12786 : 1989)
  • Range :
    Dia : 12mm, 16mm, 20mm, 25mm and 32mm OD in Class I,II & III
  • Application:
    For Drip and Mini Sprinkler System.
