Agri Pipes & Fittings

Avail from us a comprehensive range of best quality UPVC Pressure Pipes which are manufactures using high grade Plastic material accompanied by advanced technological practices. Owing to our quality oriented approach, we have carved a niche among the eminent Manufacturers of PVC Pressure Pipe Fittings. Our High Pressure Pipes are distinguished for a number of characteristics such as:

 

  • Longer life
  • Economical
  • Easy installation and operation
  • Excellent flow properties
  • Superb Elasticity
  • Specification

 

Application
  • Corrosion resistant pressure pipe.
  • Metric series sizes 20mm to 280mm for use at use at temperatures up to and including 60oC.
  • Pressure rating (2.5 Kgf/cm2 to 12. Kgf/cm2) (2.5 Bar to 16 Bar)
  • Generally resistant to most acids, bases, salts, aliphatic solutions, oxidants, and halogens.

Typical applications includes: chemical processing, plating, potable water systems, water and Water treatment, irrigation, agricultural, and other industrial applications involving corrosive fluid transfer.

Scope

Outlined specification IS-4985, ISO-4422-2 and DIN-8062. This pipe is intended for use in applications where the fluid conveyed does not exceed 60°C.

PVC Materials

The material used in the manufacture of the pipe shall be domestically produced or Imported form reputed manufacturer rigid polyvinyl chloride (PVC) compound, Resin grade K-67.

Dimensions

PVC Pressure Pipes shall be manufactured in strict accordance to the requirements of IS: 4985-2000 or as per international standard for physical dimensions and tolerances. Each production run of pipe manufactured in compliance to this standard, shall also meet or exceed the test requirements for materials, workmanship, reversion, hydrostatic pressure, resistance to external blows density, sulphated ash content as defined in IS: 4985-2000 as well as in international standard.

 

All belled-end pipe shall have tapered sockets to create an interference-type fit, which meet or exceed the dimensional requirements and the minimum socket length for pressure-type sockets as defined in IS: 4985-2000.
